## A growing public archive

TruthOutlaw gives readers one place to follow articles, documents, and events over time.

Use the **Writer Login** link at the top of the site. Once logged in, choose **Articles**, then **New Article**. Add the title, date, summary, story, sources, and an optional featured image. Selecting **Publish** creates the article and updates the home page and timeline automatically.

## Writing structure

Start with a clear summary of what happened. Follow with the timeline, supporting details, and links to original source material. Add updates to the article as new information becomes available.
